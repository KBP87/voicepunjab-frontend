Privacy

VoicePunjabAI respects your privacy and aims to handle user data carefully while providing Punjabi text-to-speech and translation services.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when you use VoicePunjabAI and how that information is used to operate and improve the platform.

Information We May Process

When you use VoicePunjabAI, the system may process text that you enter for translation or Punjabi text-to-speech generation.

Information that may be collected or stored includes:

• Text submitted for translation or speech generation
• Usage statistics such as character counts
• Technical request logs
• IP-based usage tracking for daily limits and abuse prevention

How Information Is Used

Information processed through VoicePunjabAI may be used to:

• Generate Punjabi speech audio
• Translate English text into Punjabi
• Monitor daily usage limits
• Improve performance and reliability
• Prevent misuse of the service

Data Storage and Caching

Some translated text or generated audio may be temporarily cached to improve speed, reduce repeated processing, and help control service costs.

Third-Party Services

VoicePunjabAI uses trusted third-party services such as Google Cloud Text-to-Speech and Google Cloud Translation APIs to process speech and translation requests.

These services may handle data according to their own policies and terms.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit the Contact page.
