Frequently Asked Questions

Are paid plans live yet?

Not yet. Paid plans are being prepared and will be connected with Stripe soon. For now, you can use the Free plan and contact us if you want early access.

Can I start for free?

Yes. The Free plan is available for trying Punjabi text-to-speech, translation, and the built-in Punjabi keyboard.

Will limits change later?

Yes, pricing and limits may be refined as the platform grows, especially when paid plans and Stripe billing are fully launched.
